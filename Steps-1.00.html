Visual Problem Statement
Car accidents have become widespread in all states of the United States and cause significant loss of lives each Month as well as material losses, we will analyze the accident at a traffic signal.   According to the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, in 2016 alone, 21% of the total Accidents in the US were at traffic signals. The reality is that as long as the car is so important to transportation, we cannot eliminate the accidents.
