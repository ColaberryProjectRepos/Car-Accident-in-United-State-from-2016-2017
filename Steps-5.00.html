Suggestion 5
1. Cities could ease congestion and improve safety during snowstorms by tweaking the timing of traffic lights to take road conditions into account.

2. Drivers must maintain a level of care appropriate to the weather conditions.

3. Drivers should Stop, Look, Listen. Be aware of blind spots, including those in rearview mirrors and behind windshield pillars or highway road signs.
